About the brand

Giuly's Studio presents itself as a reference point for fashion targeting this increasingly demanding public and, in just 2 years, we have consolidated our brand image in 3 shops around Wales. We have been able to provide sustainable fashion in Cardiff, Bristol and Newport and look forward to opening our new store this summer in London, targeting a new market. We take great pride in offering high-quality and eco-friendly clothing options for our customers.





Target group

Our public is characterized by adventurous young people, who are aware of the latest trends and are interested in taking care of our environment for future generations. We believe we can change the world one heart, one purchase and one item at a time.
